Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 29, 2021

CattyMod respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use CattyMod.

Information We Collect

CattyMod itself does not collect any personal data from users. Any data collection depends on the Scratch projects or extensions you use within CattyMod.

Cookies and Tracking

CattyMod does not use cookies or any tracking technologies directly.

Third-Party Services

CattyMod is built on open-source projects such as TurboWarp and PenguinMod. These projects may have their own privacy policies. CattyMod does not control third-party services and is not responsible for their data practices.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any data involved in your use of CattyMod, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

CattyMod is suitable for users of all ages, but please note that I do not knowingly collect or store any data from users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

CattyMod is a community mod and does not provide official support or contact methods.
