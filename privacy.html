Privacy Policy

Updated July 7, 2025

This privacy policy applies to all websites and services hosted under the CattyMod project, including:

  • https://cattymod.github.io
  • https://cattymod.github.io/jr
  • https://cattymod.github.io/packager

Data Collection

CattyMod may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data. This includes statistics like how often features are used, what operating system or browser is used, and other technical diagnostics (like cache hit rates).

Cloud Variables

If a project uses cloud variables, the project ID and username may be logged for up to 14 days. This helps maintain stability and detect potential abuse.

Usernames and Local Storage

A randomly generated or user-set username may be stored in your browser’s local storage. Random usernames are anonymized before being sent to the server. If you explicitly set a username, it may be logged for anti-abuse purposes.

Wi-Fi Extensions and APIs

Some Scratch extensions (like Translate, TTS, LEGO, micro:bit, etc.) may send data to the Scratch API or to CattyMod proxy APIs. For example, the Translate extension might send the original and translated text to CattyMod’s server for performance optimization. This data may be cached.

Spam Protection

In rare cases of spam or abuse, IP addresses may be temporarily logged for up to 24 hours to protect the site. This is done very rarely and only when needed.

Third-Party Stuff

This policy does not cover third-party services, external links, bots using cloud variables, custom extensions, or URL hacks. Be careful with what you click or connect.

Contact

CattyMod is a Scratch mod built using open-source tools. This privacy policy is adapted from the TurboWarp policy as of September 29th, 2021.

No official contact method is provided, as CattyMod does not operate as an organization and does not offer individual support.
